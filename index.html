Hello! This is an interactive visualization of timelines in Homestuck. As there are a lot of time shenanigans in this story, timelines are tracked per-character. Overall, time will flow from left to right but the visualization adjusts to better display the shananigans.

Things you can do:
  • click nodes for more info
  • drag nodes
  • pan
  • scroll to zoom in and out
